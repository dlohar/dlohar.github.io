About Me

Hi! I am an Assistant Professor in the Software Engineering section at the IT University of Copenhagen.

Before this, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, working with Bernhard Beckert. I obtained my PhD from Saarland University in collaboration with the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), Saarbrücken, where I was advised by Eva Darulova.
